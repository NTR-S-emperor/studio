Spy App

The Spy App allows the MC to view the girlfriend's phone. This includes her Messenger conversations, InstaPics, and OnlySlut content. The content displayed depends on "anchors" that control how far the MC can see into her phone.

Unlocking the Spy App

The Spy App is not available by default. It must be unlocked through the story.

To unlock the Spy App, use this command in the MC's Messenger conversation:

$spy_unlock

Once the MC encounters this command, the Spy App becomes accessible from the phone's home screen.

Unlocking GF's Apps

By default, when the Spy App is unlocked, only Messenger and Gallery are visible on the girlfriend's phone. InstaPics and OnlySlut must be unlocked separately:

$spy_unlock_instapics
$spy_unlock_onlyslut

These commands are used in the MC's Messenger conversations. Once called, the corresponding app will appear on the girlfriend's home screen.

Tip

Use these commands strategically to reveal apps at the right moment in your story. For example, you might want the MC to discover that his girlfriend has an OnlySlut account later in the story.

Anchor System

The Spy App uses an "anchor" system to control what content the MC can see on the girlfriend's phone. This prevents the MC from seeing future conversations or posts.

How it works

  1. Place anchor points in the girlfriend's conversations using $spy_anchor_X
  2. When the MC reaches $spy_anchor_X in his own conversation, he can see the girlfriend's content up to that anchor

Example

In the girlfriend's conversation:

Sarah
Benjamin : Hey, how are you?
gf : I'm fine, just thinking about things...
$spy_anchor_1
Benjamin : Want to grab coffee later?
gf : Sure, sounds good!
$spy_anchor_2

In the MC's conversation:

Sarah
gf : Hey $mc!
mc : Hey, what's up?
$spy_anchor_1
gf : Nothing much, just relaxing.
mc : Cool, me too.
$spy_anchor_2
How it works

When the MC reaches $spy_anchor_1 in his conversation, he can see the girlfriend's messages up to her $spy_anchor_1. He won't see the coffee invitation until he reaches $spy_anchor_2 in his own conversation.

Tip

Use anchors strategically to reveal information at the right moment in your story. This creates dramatic tension when the MC discovers what his girlfriend has been doing.

Girlfriend's Messenger

The girlfriend has her own Messenger with her own conversations. These are created the same way as the MC's conversations, in a separate folder structure.

Structure

  • stories
    • 1-My Story
      • messenger MC's messenger
      • spy GF's phone content
        • messenger
          • characters
          • talks
        • instapics
        • onlyslut

Writing GF's conversations

The girlfriend's conversations are written the same way as the MC's. However, some commands behave differently:

Command Status Notes
$talks Works Navigate between conversations
$pics, $vids, $audio Works Send media in conversations
$insta Works Unlock InstaPics posts on GF's phone
$slut Works Unlock OnlySlut posts on GF's phone
$spy_anchor_X Works Set anchor points for MC visibility
$status Works Display status messages
$delete Works Shows deleted message (by GF or her contacts)
$delete = XXXX Disabled Timed deletion not available
$react Works Add reactions to messages (displayed statically, no animation)
$thinking Works GF's inner thoughts (scroll-triggered)
$choices Disabled Not available in GF's conversations
$fake.choices Disabled Not available in GF's conversations
$lock Disabled Not available in GF's conversations
About $delete

The $delete command works in the girlfriend's conversations. When the MC views her phone, he will see "Message deleted" - indicating that either the girlfriend or one of her contacts deleted a message. This can be useful for storytelling (hiding suspicious messages, etc.).

About $thinking

The $thinking command displays the girlfriend's inner thoughts. Since the MC scrolls through her conversations freely (rather than advancing click by click like in his own Messenger), the behavior is adapted: when the MC scrolls past a thought trigger, the overlay appears and scroll is temporarily locked until he dismisses it. Use $/ to separate multiple thought bubbles - an arrow will appear to advance through them, and a close button appears on the final bubble.

Girlfriend's Social Media

The girlfriend has her own InstaPics and OnlySlut feeds, separate from the MC's. Posts are unlocked through her Messenger conversations using $insta and $slut.

Limitations

The MC is viewing the girlfriend's phone as a "spy" - he cannot interact with her apps:

  • Cannot like posts
  • Cannot comment
  • Cannot send messages
  • Can only view what she sees

Liked Posts Indicator

There is a special feature to show which posts the girlfriend has liked. In the post file, add (liked) after the like count:

Sarah
Image: photo.jpg
Text: Beautiful sunset! #nature
Likes: 24 (liked)
Comments
John = Amazing!

The (liked) indicator tells the system that the girlfriend has liked this post. The MC will be able to see this when viewing her phone.

Why this matters

This feature is specific to the girlfriend's social media. On the MC's side, he likes posts himself by interacting with them. But since the MC cannot interact with the girlfriend's apps, the (liked) indicator is the only way to show what she has liked - which can reveal important story information.
